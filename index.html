About

A neighbor, not a call center.

Silver City Systems is a one-person, locally owned tech business serving Sherrill and the surrounding Oneida County area. When you call, you talk to me — the same person who shows up at your door.

I started this business because people deserve affordable, honest tech help without having to drive to a big box store and wait in line. Whether it's a slow laptop, a dead router, or just figuring out how to stop those scam emails, I can help.

Every job comes with a quote before I start. No surprise charges. Parts are always approved by you before I order anything.
